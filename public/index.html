Meet QBot

QBot is the digital avatar of creator Marquis Bentley, developed with BotKit.

With QBot you can have natural conversations that offer more insight about the creator's resume and personal interests.

Try it out for yourself, or continue reading to learn more about the types of things you can say or ask QBot!
